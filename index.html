Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on complexity. A standard web application MVP often takes 6–8 weeks, while enterprise ML integrations or IoT deployments may range from 3–6 months. We provide a detailed roadmap during discovery.

Do you provide ongoing support after deployment?

Yes. We offer continuous DevOps support, security patching, server maintenance, and feature scaling through tailored service-level agreements.

Can you migrate our legacy systems to the cloud?

Absolutely. We specialize in refactoring legacy codebases, containerizing applications with Docker/Kubernetes, and migrating infrastructure to AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud with minimal disruption.
