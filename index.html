Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on complexity. A standard web application MVP takes 6-8 weeks, while enterprise ML integrations or IoT deployments may range from 3-6 months. We provide detailed roadmaps during discovery.

Do you provide ongoing support after deployment?

Yes. We offer continuous DevOps support, security patching, server maintenance, and feature scaling through specialized Service Level Agreements (SLAs) tailored to your business.

Can you migrate our legacy systems to the cloud?

Absolutely. We specialize in refactoring legacy codebases, containerizing applications via Docker/Kubernetes, and migrating infrastructures to AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud with zero data loss.
